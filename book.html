You’ll find the perfect place to call home in Montréal, whatever your taste, lifestyle or budget. The island has more than 25,000 rooms, with 16,000 located downtown, and many connected directly to metro stations, shopping centres and the underground pedestrian network. From major American and international chains to quaint boutique hotels, you are sure to find the perfect home-away-from-home.
